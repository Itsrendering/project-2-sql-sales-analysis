# project-2-sql-sales-analysis
SQL project analyzing sales data to calculate product and category revenue, identify top products, and track customer order activity. Focuses on JOINs, aggregation, and grouping foundational skills for junior data analysts


This project uses a small sales dataset to explore:
- Product revenue
- Category revenue
- Top products
- Customer order activity

The goal is to practice **JOINs, aggregation, and grouping** in SQL.

---

## Dataset
- customers.csv
- products.csv
- orders.csv

Tables include product prices, orders, quantities, and customer signups.

---

## Queries
1. Revenue per product
2. Revenue per category
3. Products never ordered (stretch)
4. Top 3 products by revenue
5. Orders per customer

Each query is saved in `queries/` folder.

---

## Key Insights (example)
- Laptop generates the most revenue
- Electronics category brings the highest total revenue
- Some products are never ordered (stretch)
- Customer 1 placed the most orders

---

## Notes
This project focuses on **SQL fundamentals** that a junior analyst should master.
